Motivation... The final frontier.

Cycling long distance is a journey of self-discovery. It is a challenge of the mind, body, and spirit. It is a test of endurance, perseverance, and grit. But it is also a journey of joy, freedom, and exhilaration.

When you cycle long distance, you are not just pedaling your way across the landscape. You are also pedaling your way through your own thoughts and emotions. You are confronting your fears, your doubts, and your limitations. And you are learning to overcome them.

Cycling long distance is not for everyone. It is a challenge that requires a lot of hard work, dedication, and sacrifice. But it is also a journey that is incredibly rewarding.
